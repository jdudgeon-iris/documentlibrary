Home Safety Checklist
# Item Status Plan Follow Up Date Completed
1 The yard is free of broken glass, trash, debris, and animal feces
2 Walkways are free of debris and in good repair
3 Entrance is free of clutter and debris
4 Trash is kept in covered containers and/or bagged
5 Screen doors have guards and/or locks
6 Doors have locks (out of reach of children, age dependent)
7 There are 2 easily accessible exits (at least 1 door and 1 window) free of debris in separate parts of the home
8 Safety gates at top/bottom of stairs
9 Basement doors are locked (or have safety gate)
10 Space heaters are out of reach of children, and are not near furniture, curtains, or trash cans
11 The fireplace has a cover
12 The second floor has a window ladder for escape and/or plan of escape from 2nd story
13 Family has emergency plans for fires that includes a meeting spot
14 Family has an emergency plan for tornados
15 Home has operational smoke/CO detectors on every level
16 Emergency phone numbers are posted in/near the phone; children know 911
17 Lighters/matches/ashtrays are kept out of reach of children
18 Has a fire extinguisher and is out of reach of children
19 Windows are not broken or boarded
20 Opening windows have screens
21 Blind and drapery cords are secured and out of reach
22 Windows are not opened more than 4 inches or have guards
23 Hallways and stairways are well lit
24 Unused outlets have outlet covers or are inaccessible to small children
25 Outlets and cords are not overloaded
26 Electrical cords are in good condition and are kept out of reach of children
27 Home has a heat source
28 Home has ventilation (air conditioning or windows that can open)
29 Fans have guards and are out of reach of children
30 All medications have child-proof lids and are out of reach of children
31 All purses are out of reach of children
32 Houseplants are kept out of reach of small children
33 Houses build before 1978 are tested for lead
34 Home has running water or access to water
35 Basement is free of debris or standing water
36 There is a working toilet (or outhouse as appropriate)
37 There is hot water (under 120° F) or a means to heat it
38 Infants and toddlers are not left in tubs/pools/bathrooms unsupervised
39 Heavy appliances are on a broad basis to avoid tipping
40 Scissors, knives and other sharp objects are kept out of reach of children
41 Hot pans are kept to the back of the stove and handles turn to the side
42 All cleaning products are stored in locked cabinets or out of children’s reach
43 Tools are stored in locked areas or kept out of children’s reach
44 Safe sleeping practices are used for infants/toddlers
45 All children have their own appropriate sleeping space
46 Sleepwear and bedding are seasonally and age appropriate
47 No bumper pads, pillows, soft bedding or stuffed animals in crib
48 Toys are age appropriate
49 Toys are checked for loose parts, sharp edges, or small parts – broke toys are thrown away
50 Car seats are age/weight appropriate and installed correctly
51 Infants/children are not left unattended in vehicles, especially in warmer weather
52 Litter boxes/ cages are out of reach of children
53 If animals are kept indoors, floors are free of feces
54 Home is free of pests (rodents/bugs)
55 There is a three-day food supply in the home
56 Home is free of structural concerns and other health hazards
57 Breakable items are kept out of reach of children.
58 Guns have locks or stored in locked cabinets. Ammunition is stored separately and out of the reach of children
59 Floors are free of small debris which can be placed in toddler’s mouth
60 Food given to children are cut into small non-choke able pieces
61 Infants are not left alone on changing tables, beds, sofas, or chairs
62 Parents keep hot liquids out of reach of children and do not hold children while carrying hot liquids
63 Check frequently for product recalls on toys, food, furniture, etc.
